Qbik
Qbik is a service for generating printed pages from text and photos. With it, you can easily create a menu for an institution, a collage for printing, or even a completely ready-made magazine. It is intended for small and medium-sized businesses that want to optimize the time and cost of creating corporate materials.

Task
Often, young companies experience difficulties when creating corporate materials — they need to make flyer layouts, beautifully design menus, print a corporate magazine with the results of the company's activities and perform a thousand more typographic tasks — but there simply is no time or money for this.

Is there no designer or layout designer on staff? Don't you want to spend time and money on finding and attracting a third-party person to complete the order? Do you need to make a layout urgently and do not have time to work physically? There is a way out and this is Qbik.

Decision
Qbik will save you from these difficulties. Now you don't need to have the skills of a designer, copywriter or layout designer to create the right product. Just a little attention is enough - even a beginner can handle it.

The built-in editor allows you to quickly and conveniently create materials of any nature: from a flyer to a magazine, from a one-page price list to a multi-page catalog. Choose pictures, colors, fonts - make your project unique - and download it for free in the desired format.

Primary
Our company has developed 2 mobile applications for the premium taxi service Primary. An analog of the YandexTaxi application, but designed for a higher price segment and other target audience.

The project is a modern solution for the interaction of the service with both customers and employees - passengers and drivers.

Task
The Primary premium taxi service needed to effectively organize business tasks - to create an internal platform for continuous interaction of employees regardless of their location and an external platform for working with customers.

It was necessary to simplify the work with the service from the client's side - to make an intuitive application with ordering a taxi in two clicks, as well as organize the collection of data from the target audience for further analysis.

Decision
The mobile application made it possible to effectively organize business processes. Before the development, we conducted an analysis of the target audience, defined goals, tasks and started working. When creating the application, we took into account the competitive advantages of analogues and the characteristics of the target audience, which allowed us to create a high-quality final product.

The Primary application is made in a luxury style to match the status of the company and its customers. The user-friendly and well-thought-out interface made it possible to increase user loyalty.
